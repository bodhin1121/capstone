About Me

Hi, my name is Bodhin Peterson-Smart. I am a senior at the University of Colorado Boulder studying Creative Technology and Design with minors in Engineering Management and Engineering Entrepreneurship.

Growing up with two moms, one who was an avid technology user and the other who still can't figure out how to save a pdf taught me the importance of designing for everyone. I discovered UX design my freshman year in my Design Foundations course and have been passionate about it ever since. I hope to make technology more accessible and inclusive for everyone.

In my free time, you can find me spending time in the mountains with friends and going snowboarding. Traveling to remote places to shoot photography is one of my passions, specifically landscapes and portraiture.
